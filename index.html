THE WISE METHOD


  • Merges the most important pillars of SMC, Market Profile and Market Cipher so that you can achieve confidence in the graph, leave profits aside and focus on what is most important: making a good daily PROFITABILITY.

  • Identify areas on the chart where prices have historically bounced (support) or faced selling pressure (resistance). Price action near these levels can signal potential breakouts.

  • Establish clear risk management protocols. Implement stop-loss orders to limit potential losses, and ensure proper position sizing (avoid risking too much per trade).

  • Maintain a trading journal to document your trades, including entry/exit points, rationale behind each trade, and the outcome. This helps analyze performance, identify strengths and weaknesses, and refine your strategy.

  • Research factors affecting the underlying assets (company financials, economic data, industry trends). Use this knowledge to assess value and potential future performance.
